About HTML2Canvas

Empowering developers with powerful screenshot capabilities

Our Story

HTML2Canvas represents a breakthrough in web-based screenshot technology. Born from the need to capture web content without relying on server-side processing or external services, this library has evolved into a comprehensive solution trusted by developers worldwide.

What started as a simple project to render HTML elements into canvas has grown into a sophisticated tool that handles complex layouts, CSS styling, web fonts, and images with remarkable accuracy. Today, thousands of developers integrate HTML2Canvas into their applications for reporting, documentation, thumbnails, and export functionality.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide developers with a reliable, efficient, and easy-to-use tool for capturing web content. We believe that screenshot functionality should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical background or infrastructure constraints.

We are committed to maintaining an open-source solution that prioritizes performance, accuracy, and developer experience. By continuously improving our library and responding to community feedback, we ensure that HTML2Canvas remains the go-to solution for client-side screenshot generation.

Our Vision

We envision a future where capturing and sharing web content is seamless and effortless. As web applications become increasingly complex and dynamic, the need for reliable screenshot tools will only grow. We aim to stay at the forefront of this evolution, constantly adapting to new web standards and technologies.

Our vision extends beyond just taking screenshots. We're working towards a comprehensive suite of tools that enable developers to manipulate, process, and export web content in various formats, all while maintaining the simplicity and reliability that HTML2Canvas is known for.
